EL PADRINO II: BORDER INTRUSION

Hi Nanna for the upcoming film starring Telugu actor Nani and Mrunal Thakur. Sweet and heartwarming, the almost two-minute-long teaser gave a glimpse of Nani as a single father, who shares a close bond with his daughter Mahi, played by child actor Kiara K.The teaser opens with a dinner table conversation at home between father and daughter, where Nani asks what is wrong that she is not eating her food. To this, Mahi reveals that there is a boy named Varun in her class. Her response comes as a shock to him. Later we see Mahi calling her father her best friend, and sharing that he never gets angry with her. Read more
